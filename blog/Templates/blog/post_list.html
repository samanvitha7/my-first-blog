Review: Spice Garden Bistro

Had an amazing lunch experience at Spice Garden Bistro! The ambiance was cozy, and the Paneer Butter Masala was absolutely delicious. The staff was friendly, and the prices were reasonable. Highly recommend the garlic naan!

New Find: Sushi Bay

Sushi Bay just opened near my place and it’s a must-try for sushi lovers! The Dragon Roll was fresh and beautifully presented. They even have a cute corner for ramen. Definitely coming back soon.
